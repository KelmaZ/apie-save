Kurt Knispel was born on the 20th of September 1921 in the small town of Salisfeld in Sudetenland. Sudetenland is now a part of the Czech Republic. Salisfeld was located very close to the border of Poland. Due to the unknown nature of Kurt Knispel, not much is known of this World War Two legend.

Sudetenland was occupied by Nazi Germany on October 1st, 1938. At that time Kurt was 17 years old. At a young age, Knispel began to work in the automobile industry. There he probably got his love for mechanized machines and vehicles. In 1940, the 19-year-old Knispel joined the Heer forces, more specifically the Panzer-Ersatz und Ausbildungs-Abteilung 15.
